A mother is a caretaker, a divine entity of love and protection, and ultimately, for many, a mother is a space of comfort. Though we often dream that a space is a physical and tangible thing, we forget that a space is simply that which we find ourselves in or around. Mother, through the lens of grottesca, is the journey of taking care of others, being cared for, raising children, struggling for equity, and many other issues that women face. But being a mother isn't solely a human enterprise, mothers have existed for time immemorial across nearly all species. For this entry though we will focus largely on the experience of the human mother, while making slight detours to other species of animals and their roles as mothers.
