A Map, A Story.

An exercise in free-association.

How close, or far, are we away from the evil that we resist? From the evil that feels so far removed. A Map, A Story is meant to explore the space of connection and how free association allows for the ability to construct a map that would otherwise not exist, forming connections that are as fantastical as they are real.
